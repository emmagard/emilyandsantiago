Travel & Accomodations

Flying in from out of town? You have options!

Logan Airport (Boston, MA)

Boston to Bristol, RI is about an hour and a half drive.

T.F. Green Airport (Providence, RI)

Providence to Bristol, RI is about a 30 min drive.

Your also have options for lodging!

We are recommending that our guests stay in the Middletown/Portsmouth, RI area because it is convenient to both the Newport area (a great place to spend time while you are in the area and only about a 10 min drive) as well as the wedding location (about a 15-20 min drive). We will also have shuttles set up in the Middletown/Portsmouth area for the day of the wedding so you can enjoy yourselves.

The room block availability in the area is limited so we were only able to secure one room block but there are plenty of options. Keep in mind all places will have a two night minimum bc of the holiday weekend.
