Driving Directions

From Newport: Take Route 114 North through Middletown and Portsmouth. At the junction of Rt. 114 and Rt. 24 bear left, Rt. 114 Bristol. Signs for Rt. 114 are unclear from this point so be sure to take a left at each of the next two stop lights, staying on Rt. 114. Cross the Mount Hope Bridge to enter Bristol and bear left at the fort onto Rt. 114 (Ferry Road). Blithewold is 1/4 mile on the left.

From Providence: Take Exit 7 off Interstate 195. Follow Rt. 114 South for approximately 13.5 miles through Barrington, Warren and downtown Bristol. Blithewold is located on Rt. 114 on the right.

From Boston, MA: Take Interstate 93 South to Rt. 24 South. In Fall River, MA exit onto I-15 West for one exit (approximately 1.4 miles), then return to Rt. 24 South, following signs towards Newport, RI. After approximately 7 miles, cross the Sakonnet River Bridge and then take Rhode Island exit 2 (Bristol/Mt. Hope Bridge). Go right at the end of the exit ramp and cross the Mt. Hope Bridge. Bear left at the fork onto Rt. 114 (Ferry Road). Blithewold is 1/4 mile on the left.
